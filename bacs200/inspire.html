Priyanka Chopra

A hero is someone we look up to, their actions and words inspire us to be the best version we can be.

Characteristics of my hero include:

  • Brave and Courageous they speak up for what is right
  • A kind heart that truly careers and looks after peoples lives
  • Encourages others to value others and value themselves, creating a change for the better
  • Sacrifice & commitment - their impact usually costs them everything
  • A cheerful soul brings a smile to peoples faces
  • So much love to offer that it overflows bringing happiness to the people around them

Priyanka Chopra is proud of her culture. She is so full of energy and joy that it spreads to the people around her. She is well known public figure and uses her fame to share a message that makes people stronger, motivates for a change, and brings happines. She goes out of her way to help those in need and therefore is the definition of a role model and hero.
